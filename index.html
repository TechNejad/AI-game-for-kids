Your Mission, Architect

Large Language Models (LLMs) aren't just empty vessels; they are guided by a powerful, hidden set of rules called a System Prompt. This prompt defines their personality, their goals, and their boundaries before they even see your first message.

In this experience, you will:

  • Select one of four base AI personalities.
  • Customize its core instructions to your liking.
  • Interact with your creation and observe how deeply the System Prompt shapes every single response.

Your goal is to understand the direct link between instruction and behavior. Ready to build a mind?
